History & Culture

The first Hindus arrived in Bali as early as 100 BC, but the unique culture which is so apparent to any current day visitor to Bali hails largely from neighbouring Java, with some influence from Bali’s distant animist past. The Javanese Majapahit Empire’s rule over Bali became complete in the 14th century. The unique and rich culture and traditions of Bali are actually a mixture of many influences mainly from India, China, Java, colonialism and tourism.
